Research

TagSEA is a research project of the CHISEL group at the University of Victoria, in Victoria BC, and of IBM. We hope to find effective ways to help sofware developers navigate their code more easily and to support collaboration between engineers. The following are research papers that describe how TagSEA hopes to accomplish these goal.

  • Waypointing and Social Tagging to Support Program Navigation
    Storey, M-A., Cheng, L. Bull, I., Rigby, P. CHI 2006 Work In Progress, Montreal, Canada.
  • Shared Waypoints and Social Tagging to Support Collaboration in Software Development
    Storey, M-A., Cheng, L., Rigby, P., Bull, I. CSCW 2006 (to appear), Banff, Canada.
    To be presented at CSCW 2006
