Registering TagSEA

Please register TagSEA with us at the University of Victoria. This will help us to understand the kinds of users that are using TagSEA. It will also allow you to send us information about how you are using TagSEA-information such as the tags that you have created. This information is important to us and the development of TagSEA and other tagging utilities

You may choose to fill out the information below, or to remain anonymous Simply select 'Remain Anonymous' below. Otherwise, fields marked with a are required.

You will only be asked to do this once. If you would like to register later, simply select 'Cancel' below. You can register TagSEA later via the 'Register' TagSEA' button on the TagSEA preference page.
